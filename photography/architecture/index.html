Architecture Photography

For the past year I realised the beauty of architecture. How satisfying symmetrical

architecture is and how well everything was put together. It changed my view on some things.

The beautiful part of architecture photography is that the architecture doesn't necessarily

needs to be perfect and unique.. the most simple streets can make the most beautiful pictures.

It's all about trial en error and see what works and what doesn't work. 

With architecture photography I like to take pictures on the go. I feel lucky when I have my nice Nikon camera with me and I see some nice architecture that I want to capture but most of the time the beauty of architecture takes me by surprise when I'm just there and use my Iphone camera.
