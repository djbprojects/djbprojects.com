Internship NYC

 

 

 

  On the 5th of September 2017 I started my internship at Fourth Wall Events and Beyond Times Square. Fourth Wall Events is an event management company that organises events for big bands nation wide.

Like HBO, Mac Cosmetics, Delta, Pepsico and more. After the first few weeks of my internship Fourth Wall Events partnered up with Beyond Times Square so I ended up working for both companies.

 

My role during this intership was to do research on their marketing. Next to that I also had the opportunity to work on graphic designs for their flyers, business cards, brochures, presentations and mailchimp emails.

During a couple of events I was present to take photographs that they could show to their partners and clients.
