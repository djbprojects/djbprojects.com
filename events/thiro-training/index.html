This was an event to remember!

 

 

We organised this event as part of our minor in 'Event Management'  together with our client: Thiro Training.

 

I was in charge of the 'experience' of this event and have mainly been working on getting the experience across through our communication medias. I created content for the Facebook and Instagram of Thiro Training. And took all the photographs that we have used. With this event i created most of the visuals such as itineraries, signs and banners. 

 

Next to that I also recorded and edited our promotional video's in order to get the experience across to our target audience and encourage them to come to the event. And the aftermovie as well.

 

It was a blast!
